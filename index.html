Graduation Project Case Study — EchoSense

Problem: Visually impaired and autistic individuals often struggle with independent navigation and real-time obstacle detection in unfamiliar environments.

Solution: EchoSense is a wearable device utilizing LiDAR sensors and haptic/audio feedback to provide a 360-degree awareness shield for the wearer.

Impact: Developed a functional prototype that reduces reliance on traditional canes and provides a more intuitive, hands-free navigation experience.
